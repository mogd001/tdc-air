Tasman AQ data

Statistics New Zealand

The Tasman AQ data was pulled on the 2021-03-31. We pulled the data from your daily data stream through the data pipeline for PM10 noting that Tasman used a daily inclusion threshold of 90%.

Site selection

Only relevant sites were selected for analysis - a site must have at least six complete years of data or relative season to at least 2018. We are only reporting on data from 2011.

site pollutant period_start period_end
Richmond Central at Plunket PM10 2011-01-01 2020-12-31

Exceedances

In accordance with the MfE good practise guide:

  • Annual exceedances were only computed if a site had a least 75% of complete days present in a given year. Each season also needed to have 75% of complete days present.

  • 8 hour running means were only computed if 75% of the hourly values were present (NO2 and Ozone).

  • We use exceedance guidelines from NESAQ, where there were gaps in the data we used WHO.

PM10

Daily averages were first rounded to a whole number (five rounding up in accordance with the MfE good practise guide). An annual average was computed from the daily average values.

Trend results

We ran deseasonalised TheilSen trends over monthly averages for each site and pollutant using the openair R package. We also ran trends over each seasons.

For each site that meet business rules as above, we took the lastest six years of complete data per site. A site couldn’t have a gaps larger than two years. Device changes were also noted, we took the longest running series with one device for a given site.
